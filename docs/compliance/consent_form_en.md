## What is TMT?
TMT (Triage & Monitor for Threats) is an emergency response platform that helps hospitals locate and assist vulnerable individuals during crises.

## What data do we collect?
- **Basic information**: Name, phone number, emergency contacts
- **Location**: Your GPS location (only when you share it)
- **Medical profile** (optional): Mobility status, chronic conditions, medications, special equipment needs, living situation

## How is your data used?
- To alert nearby hospitals if a crisis affects your area
- To help emergency responders find and prioritize vulnerable individuals
- To send you personalized safety alerts

## How is your data protected?
- All medical data is encrypted (AES-256)
- Access is limited to authorized medical personnel only
- Every access is logged and auditable
- Your identity is separated from your clinical data (pseudonymization)

## Your rights
- You can view all your data at any time
- You can update or correct your data at any time
- You can request deletion of all your data
- You can withdraw consent at any time
- Withdrawing consent will not affect any emergency services already dispatched
